Sean Sweeney is an award-winning professional video editor, motion graphic designer, and all-around post production pro.

With 10+ years of experience, Sean helps clients of all kinds bring their video projects to life. Whether it's a large budget corporate ad campaign, feature film, testimonal, TV/Web series and anything in between.

Sean grew up in Atlanta, Georgia where he still lives with his wife Erin.
Sean earned his film degree at the Art Institute of Atlanta, where his work recieved
"Best in Show" at his graduate portfolio show.

Sean has been the Senior Editor at Spots Films since 2012, and leads high-end post production for companies like Nickelodeon, the CDC, Kaiser Permanente and many others. A computer geek at heart, Sean builds his own computers for editing and likes doing web development in his spare time and constantly fixing his broken 3D printer.

Sean will technically and creatively work with you or your client/s from start to end to create something special.
